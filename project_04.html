-The Brief-

I recently had the opportunity to be given a live brief which involved the Walker Agency. This group task involved around the personal cream, Vagisil. The aim was to try and break the stigma surrounding personal health and engage people in conversation to normalise the product. This was a difficult task from the offset because, being male, I had next to experience with female personal products. However I went into this project with an open mind.

After some research into Vagisil and similar brands and marketing campaigns, we went into the task using metaphors surrounding female personal areas and manipulating them in a way to make them seem normal in everyday conversation.
